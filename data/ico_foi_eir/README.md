# ICO FOI/EIR Complaint-Stage Surface

## Status

This folder is reserved for the first public-record surface connected to the Correction Window Test.

Current status: **staged for data publication**.

Do not treat this folder as complete until the CSV, source map, data dictionary, caveats, and verification notes are present.

## Surface

The intended surface is the UK Information Commissioner's Office FOI/EIR complaint-stage casework and timing route, covering 2018/19 to 2024/25.

The surface is deliberately narrow.

It does not measure the whole FOI repair path.

It does not include:

- the original FOI request;
- public-authority internal review;
- compliance after decision notice;
- tribunal outcomes in detail;
- the requester's practical deadline or lived hardening point.

It is a complaint-stage segment.

## Current permitted interpretation

The working interpretation from the prior build is:

> This is not collapse. It is recovery under pressure.

That line must remain caveated until the underlying data and source verification are included here.

## Claim limits

Allowed:

- This is one measurable complaint-stage surface.
- The series can be inspected and extended.
- It may show pressure, recovery, delay, or throughput changes depending on the data.

Not allowed:

- This proves Mechanical Ethics.
- This proves repair capacity is collapsing.
- This measures the whole FOI repair clock.
- This proves public-sector correction capacity in general.

## Publication discipline

No figures or headline claims should outrun the CSV and source notes.

A reader must be able to check every number without needing private project context.
